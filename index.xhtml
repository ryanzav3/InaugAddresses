Welcome to our Presidential Inaugural Addresses Site. In this project, we aimed at analzying all 59 speeches to see what each president prioritized in writing their pieces. We hope to answer questions like who referenced the economy the most, or who priorized addressing a variety of references. Our methodology and discussion page go in depth on how we analyzed and interpreted each speech. Thanks for visiting!
